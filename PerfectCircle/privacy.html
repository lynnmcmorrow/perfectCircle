Privacy Policy

Perfect Circle · Last updated June 22, 2026

Summary

Perfect Circle does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device.

Information we collect

None. Perfect Circle does not require an account, does not connect to any server we control, and does not use third-party analytics or advertising software.

Data from Apple's own services

If the app uses any standard Apple system frameworks (for example, anonymous crash diagnostics you've opted into at the iOS level, or App Store purchase records for in-app purchases, if applicable), that data is collected and handled directly by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy — we do not receive or have access to it.

Children's privacy

Perfect Circle does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this app ever begins collecting data in the future, this page will be updated and the date above will change accordingly.
